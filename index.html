Hi! I’m Sarina Maharjan — an IT major and graphic design minor at Youngstown State University who lives at the intersection of code and creativity. I’m passionate about blending creativity with technology to create meaningful, user-friendly experiences. Whether I’m coding a website, sketching an illustration, or capturing a perfect photo, I find joy in making things that connect with people.

I’ve always been a visual thinker. From designing colorful, culture-rich illustrations rooted in my Nepali heritage to creating clean, functional layouts, I love giving ideas a visual voice. My design style leans minimal yet expressive — strong lines, vibrant colors, and always a personal touch. I’m also comfortable shifting between Illustrator, InDesign, Figma, and a bit of front-end code when needed.

Beyond the screen, I’m a people person. I also love photography, especially when I can capture the little, joyful moments — like a golden retriever mid-high-five or a candid laugh with friends. I’ve supported customer service teams during their busiest moments, keeping things smooth and organized.

I’m not just building a portfolio — I’m building a story. One project at a time, I aim to create work that reflects who I am: thoughtful, curious, and always ready to try something new. If you’re into clean design, smart tech, and a little bit of fun, we’ll probably get along just fine.

I specialize in graphic design, crafting logos, branding materials, and marketing collateral that stand out. My web design skills extend from aesthetic UI/UX principles to hands-on frontend development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience.

Beyond the screen, I'm an avid photographer, capturing moments and perspectives that evoke emotion and narrative. I believe in continuous learning and am always eager to take on new challenges that push my creative boundaries. Let's connect and create something amazing!
