Hi! I’m Sarina Maharjan.

I am based in Youngstown, OH and I love blending code and creativity. Whether I’m designing posters, developing a website, or snapping photos of dogs mid-high-five, I’m all about creating meaningful, user-friendly experiences. Rooted in my Nepali heritage, my design style is minimal yet vibrant — clean lines, bold color, and a personal touch. I’m fluent in Illustrator, InDesign, Figma, and a bit of front-end code, and I thrive where tech meets storytelling. Outside the screen, I’m a people person who’s all about having a good time — and I always bring good vibes and curiosity to every project.

If you're into clean design, memorable photos, and a dash of fun, let's connect!
